About Us

PT Pranala Digital Transmaritim is a leading company specializing in maritime technology solutions. We combine advanced digital tools with deep maritime expertise to help shipping and logistics businesses operate more efficiently, safely, and sustainably. Our foundation is built on innovation, precision, and a deep understanding of the challenges faced by the maritime industry in today’s fast-changing world.

Our vision is to be a trusted partner for digital transformation in the maritime sector. We aim to empower ship owners, operators, and port authorities with the right technology to make informed decisions, optimize their resources, and remain competitive in the global market. At Pranala, technology is not just about automation — it is about creating meaningful impact for people and industries.

We provide a wide range of services tailored to the needs of modern shipping and logistics. From innovative software for vessel management, to IoT-based monitoring systems for maritime assets, and consulting services for digital transformation, our solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with existing operations. Every service we deliver is grounded in practicality, scalability, and long-term sustainability.

What sets us apart is our unique blend of maritime knowledge and digital expertise. Our team consists of professionals who understand both the complexities of maritime operations and the potential of cutting-edge technologies. This dual competency enables us to craft solutions that are not only technically advanced but also operationally relevant, addressing real-world pain points in shipping, safety, and logistics.

Looking ahead, we remain committed to pushing the boundaries of maritime technology. Our mission is to continuously develop smarter tools, adopt emerging innovations such as AI, big data, and predictive analytics, and contribute to a greener, more sustainable maritime industry. PT Pranala Digital Transmaritim is not just building technology — we are shaping the future of maritime excellence.
